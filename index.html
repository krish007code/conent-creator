Full job description

Company: Zingy Gifts
Job Type: Full-Time / Internship

About the Role

Are you a confident, camera-loving storyteller? Do you know what makes a Reel go viral? We are looking for the new Face of Zingy Gifts! You will be the personality our customers see every day—hosting videos, unboxing our personalized products, taking followers behind the scenes, and managing our social media presence. If you want to build a personal brand while growing a business, this is your stage.

Key Responsibilities

1. On-Camera Hosting (The "Face" Role)

  • Be the primary on-camera presenter for all Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.
  • Host engaging videos: product unboxings, studio tours, "Day in the Life," client gifting stories, and trend-based skits.
  • Confidently present in both Hindi and English (Hinglish is perfect) to connect with our audience.
  • Collaborate with the video editor to plan shots and deliver punchy, engaging scripts/talking points.

2. Social Media Strategy & Management

  • Schedule and publish content across Instagram, YouTube & other Channels.
  • Engage with our community: reply to comments and DMs in your natural voice to build a loyal following.
  • Track which videos perform well and double down on what works.

3. Brand Representation

  • Occasionally represent Zingy Gifts at client sites or events (if comfortable) to shoot BTS content.
  • Ensure the brand's tone is consistent, fun, and trustworthy.

Who You Are (Requirements)

  • Camera Confidence: You are absolutely comfortable being filmed and speaking naturally to a camera. (Stiff, scripted readings won't work—we need natural energy).
  • Language: Fluent in Hindi and English (spoken). You can switch between them effortlessly.
  • Trend-Savvy: You are addicted to Instagram Reels and YouTube. You know the trending audios, formats, and memes before anyone else.
  • Basic Content Knowledge: You understand aspect ratios (vertical vs. horizontal), hashtags, and posting schedules.
  • Willing to Learn: You don't need to be a pro editor, but you should be open to directing the editor on what cuts you need.

Bonus Skills (Good to Have)

  • You already run a small personal page (even 500 followers) and understand the algorithm.
  • Basic video editing skills (CapCut/ Edit etc) to make quick rough cuts.

What We Offer

  • A Platform to Build Your Personal Brand: You will be the face—we will promote you alongside our brand.
  • Creative Freedom: We don't do boring corporate scripts. We want your ideas.
  • Professional Equipment: High-quality camera and setup provided.
