Details

Naraway is building modern digital solutions across AI, IT services, and startup consulting. We are looking for UI/UX Design Interns who can design clean, scalable, and product-ready interfaces — not just pretty screens.

  • This is a hands-on internship where you will work directly on live projects, including web platforms, dashboards, landing pages, and internal tools.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity UI screens
  • Create responsive web and mobile-first designs
  • Conduct basic UX research and competitor analysis
  • Improve usability and conversion rates of existing products
  • Work closely with developers to ensure pixel-perfect implementation
  • Maintain design systems and reusable components

Required Skills:

  • Strong knowledge of Figma (mandatory)
  • Understanding of UX principles and user psychology
  • Basic knowledge of responsive design
  • Wireframing & prototyping
  • Design systems & component thinking
  • Ability to take feedback and iterate quickly

Good to Have:

  • Experience with SaaS dashboards
  • Knowledge of Webflow or Framer
  • Understanding of AI product interfaces
  • Portfolio with 2–3 real projects

Who Should Apply:

  • College students or freshers serious about product design
  • Individuals looking to build real-world SaaS experience
  • Designers who want execution exposure, not theoretical learning
  • This internship focuses on execution, speed, and product thinking. High performers may receive a full-time offer or extended contract.
